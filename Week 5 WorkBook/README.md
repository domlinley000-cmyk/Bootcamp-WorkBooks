# Week 5 Summary

In **Week 5**, I developed my understanding of **data protection laws**, **cloud computing concepts**, and **Microsoft Azure**. I explored how organisations can securely manage customer and business data while transitioning from manual systems to cloud-based solutions.

---

## Key Learnings & Projects

### Cloud Computing Fundamentals
* **Real-World Application:** I researched what cloud computing does in real-world environments and how it benefits businesses. 
* **Core Advantages:** I compared cloud computing with traditional on-premises infrastructure and explained advantages such as scalability, cost efficiency, remote access, and automated backups.
* **Provider Comparison:** I also researched and compared major cloud providers, including **Microsoft Azure**, **Amazon Web Services**, and **Google Cloud Platform**. This included outlining their features, functions, and typical business use cases in a comparison table.

### Cloud Models & Service Offerings
* **Service Models:** I researched and explained different cloud service models: **IaaS** (Infrastructure as a Service), **PaaS** (Platform as a Service), and **SaaS** (Software as a Service).
* **Deployment Models:** I also explored cloud deployment models, including **public, private, hybrid, and community cloud**, and explained how each could be used in a business environment.

### Data Protection & Legal Compliance
A key focus was understanding UK data laws and cybersecurity legislation. I researched and applied:
* **The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)**
* **The Data Protection Act 2018**
* **The Computer Misuse Act 1990**
* **The Police and Justice Act 2006**
* **Impact:** This helped me understand how organisations must lawfully collect, store, and protect personal data, particularly customer information.

### Azure Labs & Certification Practice
* **Hands-on Labs:** I completed hands-on labs in Azure exploring relational and non-relational data, data analytics services, cloud storage solutions, and identity and access management.
* **Certification:** I also completed the **DP-900 (Azure Data Fundamentals)** practice exam and achieved a score of **80%**, demonstrating my understanding of core Azure data concepts.

### 1500-Word Case Study: “Paws & Whiskers”
The main assessment was a structured 1500-word report analysing how a small pet shop could transition from spreadsheets to Azure. Within the report, I:
* Identified relevant **data protection laws** affecting customer data.
* Recommended **Azure services** for data storage and analysis.
* Designed a **relational database model** including tables, primary keys, and relationships.
* Suggested appropriate data formats (**CSV, JSON, Parquet**).
* Proposed **security measures**, backup strategies, and disaster recovery solutions.
* Recommended **Power BI integration** for reporting and data visualisation.
